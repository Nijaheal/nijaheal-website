About Us

NijaHeal, founded by Emiola Michael Tosin, is a nonprofit based in Zaria, Kaduna State. We're committed to supporting Nigerians facing addiction, mental health challenges, and emotional trauma. Through dedicated bootcamps, peer counseling, and awareness initiatives, we empower individuals to heal and thrive.

Our Programs

  • Mental Health Bootcamps: Intensive 30-day journeys guiding participants toward recovery.
  • Peer Support & Counseling: Safe, confidential spaces for healing and expression.
  • Advocacy & Awareness: Community campaigns to break stigma and promote mental well-being.
  • Skill Development: Training and workshops for long-term personal growth.
